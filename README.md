Inventory Management System
Project Description
The Inventory Management System is a web-based application designed to manage and track products, suppliers, purchases, and sales efficiently.
This system helps businesses maintain accurate stock records, reduce manual work, and improve inventory control.
The application allows the admin to add products, manage suppliers, update stock, and track purchase and sales transactions. It also provides low stock alerts and reports to help in decision making.
The system improves accuracy, efficiency, and data management compared to traditional manual inventory methods.

 Features
1️⃣ Admin Login

Secure login system for administrator
Only authorized users can access the system

2️⃣ Product Entry

Add new products
Edit or delete product details
Store product price, quantity, and category

3️⃣ Supplier Management

Add supplier details
Update supplier information
Maintain supplier contact information

4️⃣ Stock Update

Update product quantity
Automatically change stock after purchase or sales

5️⃣ Purchase Management

Record purchased products from suppliers
Increase stock automatically

6️⃣ Sales Management

Record product sales
Reduce stock automatically

7️⃣ Low Stock Alert

Shows notification when product quantity is low
Helps prevent stock shortages

8️⃣ Reports & Analytics

Generate sales reports
Generate purchase reports
View stock details
